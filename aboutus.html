About Us!

We are a small group of friends who first started out as classmates for our thesis. We then went on and saw the opportunity in the coffee industry and we started our path towards making our very own establishment. After graduating we decided to continue on this business as this was a great bonding that we had and we are also learning more and more about the industry along the way.

Mission

We strive to make every cup of coffee with the most love for the community

Vision

To establish our coffee as a dominant coffee provider and establish meaningful connections with our community.
